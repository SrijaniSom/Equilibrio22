About

"Equilibrio is the annual techno-management fest of Guru Ghasidas Vishwavidyalaya, Bilaspur. Initiated in 2013, Equilibrio has served as a platform for technology enthusiasts to showcase their intelligence, innovation and ingenuity. The fest brings together people from all walks of life, comprising students, professionals and the general public. Equilibrio organizes a wide range of events encompassing different genres of technology and management via competitive events, motivating guest talks, workshops, cultural programs and exhibitions. The aim of Equilibrio is to provide the technically inclined Indian youth with a national level medium to recognize and reward their passion, ideas and skills as well as to develop them. The word 'Equilibrio' means balance. Acknowledging this, every year Equilibrio promotes a natural or social cause to 'balance' technology with nature and society. Since its launch, Equilibrio has received enormous participation from all over the country and has become one of the best in central India. So, tell everyone, mark your calendars and gear up for Equilibrio 2022."
